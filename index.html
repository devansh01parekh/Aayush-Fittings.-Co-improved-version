Welcome to Aayush Fittings. Co.

About Us

Aayush Fittings was founded 25 years ago by Jatin Parekh with a vision to provide high-quality fittings and accessories. Specializing in the manufacturing of runners, sliders, and zippers, Aayush Fittings has built a reputation for delivering durable, reliable, and top-notch products. Over the years, we have remained committed to offering our clients exceptional service, competitive prices, and products that meet the highest standards. Our dedication to quality and customer satisfaction has made us a trusted name in the industry, and we continue to innovate to meet the evolving needs of our customers.

Our Services

At Aayush Fittings, we specialize in the manufacturing of high-quality runners, sliders and zippers. With a strong presence across India, we ensure timely and reliable delivery of our products nationwide. Our facility is equipped with advanced machinery, including plating machines, fitting machines, and molding machines, to produce precision-engineered products that meet the highest standards. We take pride in offering exceptional service, combining great quality, reliability, and trust to ensure complete customer satisfaction. Whether you need bulk orders or custom solutions, we are committed to exceeding your expectations.

Our Founder

Jatin Mahendra Parekh, the visionary founder and proprietor of Aayush Fittings. Co., has been the driving force behind the company's success for the past 25 years. A graduate of Gurukul High School, where he earned his high school diploma, Jatin went on to complete his B.Com degree, laying a strong foundation for his entrepreneurial journey. At just 22 years of age, with a keen eye for opportunity and an unwavering passion for quality, Jatin founded Aayush Fittings. What started as a small venture soon blossomed under his leadership, transforming into a well-established name in the manufacturing of zippers, sliders, and runners. Through his relentless dedication, innovative mindset, and commitment to quality, Jatin has steered Aayush Fittings to remarkable heights, earning trust and recognition in the industry. His entrepreneurial spirit, along with a deep understanding of customer needs, continues to inspire the growth and success of the company.
